Get Personal

Feel closer to the artists you love and support them directly, without any annoying middlemen.

Buy and listen to your favorite music and videos, discover new artists, connect with other fans and watch amazing live stream performances anywhere in the world.

Watch

Watch live performances-your favorite indie artist playing live from their garage or playing on the other side of the world, ArtistFan gives you a front row seat to live events, music videos, backstage footage and much more!

Shop

Every track, album or video you buy on ArtistFan is purchased directly from that artist and they get the lion share of the money.
Music purchased on ArtistFan is DRM free and contains metadata compatible with iTunes and mobile devices, simple!

Connect

Be a part of a community solely focused on directly helping artists. Connect with other fans, stay informed and express your passion about music and the artists you love.

Livestream Pay-Per-View

Perform virtually anywhere in the world. With Artistfan's livestream pay-per-view feature, you can perform for your fans from any stage around the world, including your living room! Imagine the possibilities of giving your fanbase access to any show you are doing at anytime!

Build a Fanbase

The Fan Finder tool is available to you at no extra charge. It provides great analytical data allowing you to better target your fans. With ArtistFan you can target specific locations for direct marketing to optimize reach when you're on the road or have a new release.

Set Up Shop

Instantly start selling your albums, singles and videos to a global audience.

There are no initial set up costs or revolving fees. You set your own price, it's your music; whether it's for free or a charge, it's totally up to you.

Making a Living

The days of having the price of your product dictated to you is over! Your profile is your personal music store. Set your own prices for your albums, tracks, videos and live streams at levels you think your fans can afford.

Artists are given a "wallet" that automatically tallies how much money they are earning in real time. Artists can track sales, monitor their transaction history and request payouts at anytime.
